The KLR 650 is a dual sport motorcycle. Introduced by Kawasaki in 1987, the KLR650 quickly gained a reputation for being reliable transportation
that one can count on. Whether you are riding across a frozen mountain pass, miles from nowhere in the desert, of just running errands around
town, the KLR 650 will get you into and out of the best of the worst conditions.
