The Kawasaki KLR650

The Kawasaki KLR650 is a dual sport motorcycle first introduced to the marked in 1987. It remained mostly unchanged untill 2008, when Kawasaki introduced the second generation KLR650. usdate suspension, brakes, and body styling made the bioke look a bit more modern while supporting the same old 80's tech. Despite the lack in innovation with the KLR 650, it maintains a hardcore cult following. Complete with meetups and ride, parts swapmeets, and online forums.
